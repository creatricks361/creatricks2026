EnviroSpark

A competition that ignites the passion of young minds to explore, understand, and champion environmental issues.

EnviroSpark

Competition
Overview

This competition consists of two intense rounds designed to test knowledge, speed, and teamwork.

01

Round 1: The Brain Sprint (Mass Elimination)

Format: Team-Based MCQ Challenge | Duration: 60 Minutes

In this opening round, every registered team will receive a comprehensive question paper containing 100 mind-bending multiple-choice questions. The objective is to huddle with your teammates, divide and conquer, and solve as many questions as possible before the buzzer sounds. Speed, accuracy, and seamless communication are your only weapons. Only the highest-scoring teams will survive the cut, with finalists being announced during the lunch break via a display in the auditorium.

02

Round 2: The Amphitheatre Showdown (Rapid Fire)

Format: Live Audience Stage Round | Capacity: 5 Teams

The qualifying teams will move from the testing halls to the energetic arena of the Amphitheatre for a high-octane, lightning-fast Rapid Fire round. You will face the quizmaster live, where hesitation is your biggest enemy. Success in this final stage requires instant reflexes and absolute trust in your team's instincts. Competing in front of a roaring crowd, every second will dictate the difference between victory and elimination.

Guidelines &
Constraints

01

Team Requirements

Students from grades 8-10 are eligible. Teams must be of 2-3 members.

02

Fairness

Use of mobile phones, smart watches, tablets, or any electronic devices is strictly prohibited during the quiz. Participants may not communicate with the audience during the event.

03

Question Types

Questions may include MCQs, direct answers, image identification, logos, videos, or current environmental affairs.Questions may include both national and international environmental topics.
