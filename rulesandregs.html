Official Event Code of Conduct

Rules & Regulations

Please review the protocol framework below. Maintaining spatial discipline and respect for the host campus ensures a safe, fair, and professional tournament environment for all schools.

Campus Zoning & Movement

Designated Venues

Participants must remain within their designated event venues and authorised common areas at all times.

Restricted Zones

Wandering into classrooms, laboratories, staff rooms, administrative areas, or other restricted zones is strictly prohibited.

Chain of Command & Operations

Institutional Respect

Participants must not disrupt regular school activities or interfere with school property.

Personnel Directives

Follow the instructions of volunteers, logistics team members, teachers, security personnel, and organisers at all times.

Loitering Enforcement

Any participant found loitering in unauthorised areas may be escorted back to their designated venue by the Logistics Team.

Enforcement & Violations

Repeated violations or failure to comply with instructions may result in warnings, deduction of marks, disqualification, or removal from the event premises.

Professional Standards

Participants are expected to conduct themselves responsibly and represent their schools with professionalism and respect.

Need Help?

For registration, spatial assignments, or protocol-related queries, reach out directly to the team.
