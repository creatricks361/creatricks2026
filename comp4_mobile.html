Competition Overview

Participants are tasked with designing a modular structure that can physically transform in response to environmental stimuli. The goal is to merge the elegance of classical proportions with the unpredictability of organic movement.

Submissions will be judged on three pillars: Aesthetic Cohesion, Mechanical Innovation, and Emotional Resonance. We are looking for designs that feel both inevitable and impossible.

  • layers 3D Parametric CAD Documentation
