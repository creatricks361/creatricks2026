Official Participation Handbook

Entry Guidelines

Please read these guidelines carefully before arriving at Creatricks 2026. Following the instructions below will help ensure a smooth registration, verification, and participation experience.

Registration Requirements

School ID

Every participant must carry a valid school ID card and the letter from the school confirming their permission to participate. This letter is especially mandatory if the teacher is not accompanying the students to the event.

Registration Proof

Carry your confirmation email or registration screenshot.

Payment Receipt

Keep a copy of your payment confirmation available.

Dress Code

Accepted Formal Attire: Formal shirts, blouses, or collared tops. Formal trousers, dress pants, or ankle-length formal skirts. Blazers, waistcoats, or formal jackets (optional). School uniform may be worn unless otherwise specified. Footwear: Formal shoes, loafers, or clean closed-toe footwear. Sneakers may be permitted if they are plain, clean, and professional-looking. Not Permitted Ripped, distressed, or excessively oversized clothing. Crop tops, off-shoulder tops, backless outfits, or excessively revealing attire. Shorts, beachwear, or sleepwear. Flip-flops, slippers, or casual sandals. Clothing with inappropriate slogans, images, or advertisements. The Organizing Committee reserves the right to request participants to modify their attire if it is deemed unsuitable for the event or school environment

Reporting Instructions

Arrival Time

Participants should report at least 30 minutes before their event begins.

Verification Desk

All participants must complete verification before entering competition zones.

Late Entry

Late arrivals may not be allowed to participate once judging or activities have started.

Campus Rules

For more info, visit our rules and regulations page.

Discipline

Participants must follow all instructions from volunteers, heads and the organizing committee. Failure to comply may lead to disqualification.

Respect Property

Any damage to school property may result in disqualification.

Restricted Areas

Entry is permitted only in designated competition and visitor areas.

No Misconduct

Misconduct, including cheating, harassment or vandalism, will lead to immediate disqualification and can result in being removed from the event venue.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I participate in multiple events?

No.

Will certificates be provided?

Yes. Participation and winner certificates and awards will be distributed.

Can teachers accompany students?

Yes. Teachers may accompany participants according to event policy.
