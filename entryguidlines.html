Official Participation Handbook

Entry Guidelines

Please read these guidelines carefully before arriving at Creatricks 2026. Following the instructions below will ensure a smooth registration, verification and participation experience.

Registration Requirements

School ID

Every participant must carry a valid school ID card.

Registration Proof

Carry your confirmation email or registration screenshot.

Payment Receipt

Keep a copy of your payment confirmation available.

Reporting Instructions

Arrival Time

Participants should report at least 30 minutes before their event begins.

Verification Desk

All participants must complete verification before entering competition zones.

Late Entry

Late arrivals may not be allowed to participate once judging or activities have started.

Campus Rules

Discipline

Participants must follow all instructions from volunteers, heads and the organizing committee. Failure to comply may lead to disqualification.

Respect Property

Any damage to school property may result in disqualification.

Restricted Areas

Entry is permitted only in designated competition and visitor areas.

No Misconduct

Misconduct, including cheating, harassment or vandalism, will lead to immediate disqualification and can result in being removed from the event venue.
