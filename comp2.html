Hue Nova

HueNova is more than a painting competition—it is a creative platform for young artists to express themselves. Participants will explore thought-provoking ideas related to society and the environment, encouraging the expression of concepts through color. The goal of this event is not only to showcase artistic skill, but also to inspire conversation, evoke emotion, and create meaningful impact. Art has the power to bring change, and HueNova aims to be the spark that begins it.

Painting Sketching July 24th

Competition Overview

Participants will receive the competition themes before the event for preparation and idea development. On the day of the event, students must create their artwork live within the allotted time based on the selected theme. Students are encouraged to showcase originality, artistic skill, and creative interpretation through their work.

All entries must demonstrate a clear connection to SDG 7 – Affordable and Clean Energy. Participants should creatively incorporate themes of renewable energy, energy conservation, sustainability, innovation, or responsible energy use through their designs, performances, products, artwork, presentations, or concepts. The relevance to SDG 7 will be considered as part of the judging criteria.

Constraints

01

Eligibility

Students of grades 9-12 are eligible. Individual registration only.

02

Originality

All work must be original and created specifically for Creatricks 2026. Prior competition entries are not permitted.

03

Art mediums

Only traditional art mediums are allowed. Digital submissions will not be accepted.

Content

Given artwork must be school appropriate and remain respectful. Judges reserve the right to disqualify submissions that do not meet these criteria.
