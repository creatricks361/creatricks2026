LumiNara

From concept building to final presentation, Luminara celebrates confidence, aesthetics, and the art of storytelling through fashion. Whether your style is bold, elegant, futuristic, vintage, or experimental, this is your chance to bring your vision to life on stage. Step into the spotlight with LUMINARA, the ultimate fashion styling challenge at the Creatricks 2026, where creativity meets individuality.

Luminara

Competition
Overview

01

Round 1 - The Vision Forge

The competition begins online. Teams must conceptualize and submit an original mood board along with fashion sketches inspired by the theme. Creativity, storytelling, innovation, and execution will determine who advances. Only the most striking and well-developed concepts will survive this elimination round and secure a place in the finals.

02

Round 2 – Runway Reveal

Finalists will bring their vision to life on stage through a dynamic presentation of their designed looks. Participants must command the runway with confidence, styling precision, and impactful choreography that enhances the concept behind their creation. This round tests not only fashion sense, but stage presence and performance ability.

03

Round 3 – The Final Twist

In the ultimate challenge, participants will create a unique modification to their outfits, incorporating a show stopper. Teams must think creatively under pressure to elevate and transform their final presentation while maintaining cohesion, originality, and visual impact. This round will test adaptability, innovation, and true styling mastery.

Guidelines &
Constraints

01

Punctuality

Teams must report to the venue on time. Late entries may not be accommodated.

02

Props

Dangerous props, offensive symbols, or inappropriate content are strictly prohibited.

03

Digital Submission

Email entries with the subject [Event Name] – School – Team. Include all member details (names, grades, contact) in the body, and attach your portfolio (PDF, PPT/PPTX, or JPEG/PNG).

04

Originality & AI

All work must be entirely original. Plagiarism, copied content, and AI-generated designs, concepts, mood boards, or sketches are strictly prohibited and will lead to disqualification.

05

Deadlines & Edits

Late entries will not be considered without prior committee approval. Only one entry is allowed per team; if multiple are sent, only the latest version will be evaluated.

06

IP Rights & Selection

Designers retain full IP rights, but Creatricks may exhibit works. Incomplete/mismatted entries will be rejected. Shortlisted teams will be notified via email.

Winter Solstice

Game of Thrones

Step into a high-fantasy, mythical European winter inspired by Nordic folklore and epic sagas. This theme captures the fierce, rugged beauty of a frozen world where clans endure the biting cold, howling winds, and an endless night. But beneath the ice lies a whimsical, ancient magic. The solstice is the ultimate battle between the freezing dark and the birth of a new dawn, celebrating survival, camaraderie, and the return of the sun.

Winter Solstice 1

Echoes of The Cosmos

Celestial and Spatial Phenomena

A breathtaking odyssey into the deep, mysterious expanse of the universe. This theme explores the magnificent phenomenon of outer space, ranging from the silent birth of a nebula to the blinding light of a supernova. It bridges the gap between hard science and surreal artistry, portraying the universe as a living, glowing entity of infinite wonder.

Echoes of Cosmos 2

Drapes of India

Folklore, Heritage, and Traditions

A grand, cinematic celebration of India's cultural soul told through its legendary textiles. This theme treats fabrics not just as clothing, but as historical scrolls carrying ancient folklore, regional pride, and unparalleled craftsmanship. It honors the weavers, the storytellers, and the timeless stories of our rich past that make Indian heritage a vibrant, living masterpiece.
