Morphis

A creative innovation event that challenges students to think beyond imagination and transform ideas into impactful designs and solutions. a platform for students to showcase their originality, teamwork, and practical thinking while addressing modern-day concepts and themes. Participants will explore design thinking, environmental awareness, and creative expression through exciting competitive rounds.

Competition Overview

Round 1 – Design Creation

In the first round, participants will have to come up with a concept for the product they intend to create and make an online presentation on the same. The aim is to judge contestants based on their ability to articulate their ideas properly and come up with something practical yet unthought of. This will be a preliminary elimination round.

Round 2 – Idea presentation

Participants will come up on stage one after the other to present a prototype of the concept they had previously concocted. They must explain the inspiration, purpose and functionality behind their idea to the judges. Here, the contestants’ problem-solving skills and confidence will shine.

Round 3 - Rapid Innovation Round

In the final round, the shortlisted participants will be tested on their adaptability and design thinking skills. A surprise theme will be revealed on the spot and the teams will have to brainstorm and develop a product idea within the allotted time. The final evaluation will be based on originality, practicality, creativity, presentation and the relevance of the product to the theme.
