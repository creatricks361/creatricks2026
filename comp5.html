A M P L I F I R E

In a classic battle of the bands, our young talents take the centre stage and ignite a fire of passion within our hearts through the language of music. From head-banging hits to tunes that strike at our heartstrings, participants will be center stage with original performances to invigorate the crowd. In this competition, any competitor will be as good as a winner as long as they can move the hearts of the audience.

July 24th, 2026

Competition Overview & Rules

Participants must bring their own instruments and perform a song based on the theme announced prior to the event. Only songs originally performed by bands are permitted; Bollywood tracks and other non-band genres are not allowed. Each team will have 2 minutes to showcase their talent. Performances will be judged on originality, execution , and overall impact.

Amplifire celebrates the energy, passion, and collaboration of live music, giving bands the perfect platform to take the stage and make their mark
