A M P L I F I R E

In a classic battle of the bands, young talents take the stage and ignite a passion for music in every listener. From high-energy performances to songs that touch the heart, participants will showcase original work and energize the crowd. In this competition, every performer has the chance to leave a lasting impression.

July 24th, 2026

Competition Overview & Rules

Participants must bring their own instruments and perform a song based on the theme announced before the event. Only songs originally performed by bands are permitted; Bollywood tracks and other non-band genres are not allowed. Each team will have 2 minutes to showcase their talent, and performances will be judged on originality, execution, and overall impact.

Amplifire celebrates the energy, passion, and collaboration of live music, giving bands the perfect platform to take the stage and make their mark.

All entries must demonstrate a clear connection to SDG 7 – Affordable and Clean Energy. Participants should creatively incorporate themes of renewable energy, energy conservation, sustainability, innovation, or responsible energy use through their designs, performances, products, artwork, presentations, or concepts. The relevance to SDG 7 will be considered as part of the judging criteria.
