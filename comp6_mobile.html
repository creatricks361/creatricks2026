PhoenixAlley

Contestants get a chance to try their hand at retail and earn a profit by making original products and selling them. This is the perfect opportunity to try your hand at marketing– Can you convince customers that your product is worth spending their precious money on? Phoenix Alley is a fun opportunity to encourage young entrepreneurs to try and start businesses of their own for a single day.

The submission deadline for the backtracks is 20th July, 2026

Phoenix Alley

Competition Overview

Students participating in the stall activities are expected to maintain professionalism, creativity, and discipline throughout the event. Stalls may include food, games, handmade products, art, crafts, accessories, or other student-led business ideas approved by the organizers.


All entries must demonstrate a clear connection to SDG 7 – Affordable and Clean Energy. Participants should creatively incorporate themes of renewable energy, energy conservation, sustainability, innovation, or responsible energy use through their designs, performances, products, artwork, presentations, or concepts. The relevance to SDG 7 will be considered as part of the judging criteria.

Themes – Grade 7-9

  • check_circle Maximum 2 stalls per school are allowed.
  • check_circle Each stall must have a team of 3-5 students.
  • check_circle All business items must be safe and eco-friendly.

Guidelines &
Constraints

01 / ELIGIBILITY

Open to all students, faculty, and alumni of accredited design schools. Teams are limited to a maximum of 4 members.

02 / ORIGINALITY

All work must be original and created specifically for Creatricks 2026. Prior competition entries are not permitted.

03 / SUBMISSION

Digital portfolios must be submitted in PDF format (A3, max 20 pages). Video content should not exceed 180 seconds.

04 / IP RIGHTS

Designers retain full IP rights. Creatricks reserves the right to exhibit and publish works for promotional purposes.
